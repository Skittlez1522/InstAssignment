Assignment Checking

We look forward to checking your assignments, but to do so, you will have to give us a little bit of information so we know how to check them. We don't ask for any personal information other than a way to contact you. This, of course, is just so we can give you feedback on the assignment we are checking for you. If you want your assignment checked, please fill out the form below. Once you fill out the form, we will contact you asking for the assignment you would like us to check.

We also offer assignment checking with a video/voice call. With this service, you are not only getting your assignments checked, but you are also able to schedule a video call or voice call with us. In this call, we will go through the questions you got wrong and walk you through them if you want to. We will always try to do the calls around your schedule as to not inconvenience you. If you have any questions or concerns, you can always contact us through any of our social media pages or on the contact page of our website.
