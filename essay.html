Essay Editing

Writing essays is hard enough, but having to proof read and edit your essay after you write it is something you should do, but you probably don't. So how about you leave it up to us. We will read through your essay, add notes and help with any grammatical or punctuation errors you might have. We will also suggest different ways to improve your essay, whether it be rewriting a sentence or changing the order in which you write about your topic. Of course, it is always your choice to either take these suggestions or not.

We also offer the option to do the proof reading and editing with you in a video/voice chat. With this option, you are able to talk to us about the edits, notes, and suggestions we made on your essay. We will walk you through all of the edits and notes we made and explain our suggestions.
